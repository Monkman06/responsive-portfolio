I am currently looking to begin a career as a Front End web development/ designer, particularly in the New York City area. As an avid chess player and amateur historian, I find that Front End development suits my highly analytical nature, exacting attention to detail, and uncanny ability to think outside the box. On the surface, studying history and writing code appear incompatible, but they are remarkably similar: Both require minute attention to detail, clearly defined objectives, precise documentation, and remain objective. Coding and chess require thinking outside the box. For example, only a few well-placed pieces can secure victory. While designing a role player game at Rutgers Coding Boot Camp, I could not find stock photos that matched the fantasy theme and had to improvise by scouring Google images, taking screen shots, converting them to jpegs, and linking them to the css. Now the game looks amazing.
