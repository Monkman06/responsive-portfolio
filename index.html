Work History

I am in the process of changing careers from fitness to web development. For the last few years, I worked as a personal trainer at several gyms in New Jersey and New York City. Initially, fitness was a rewarding field. However, the uncertainty of signing on clients, the drastic shifts in new business depending on the season , and the need for more predictable hours necessitated a change; I enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp after consulting a career advisor at my alma mater. I look forward to pursuing a career as front end web developer: It affords a greater amount of creative liberty than back end.

   

Education

I graduated from high school in 2005 and graduated from college in 2008 & 2010. I have an Associates of Science Degree in Criminal Justice from County College of Morris and a Bachelors of Arts in History from the University of Maine in Orono. In five years of college, I only missed only one class and that was because I was still memorizing the schedule. I also have two primary personal trainer certifications and four specialties.
